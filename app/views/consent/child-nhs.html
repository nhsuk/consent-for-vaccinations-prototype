An NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.

You can find it:

  • in their personal child health record (red book)
  • using the Find your NHS number service
  • on any letter from the NHS like a prescription or appointment letter
  • by calling your GP surgery and asking them for the number
  • by logging in to a GP practice online service
